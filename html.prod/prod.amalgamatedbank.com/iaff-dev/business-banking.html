We have what you need for your Local Affiliate or business

We offer affordable and professional services to our community and your Local Affiliate or small business. That includes the right products at the right time throughout your organization's development.

Business checking accounts

We offer three checking account options to accommodate your Local Affiliate or small business’ unique needs.

  • IAFF-FC Small Business Checking – a great account for organizations or businesses with relatively low transaction volume
  • IAFF-FC Specialty Interest Checking – an account specifically created for sole proprietor, non-profit, and government/municipalities
  • IAFF-FC Commercial Checking – an account designed for larger businesses, allowing you to pay only for the banking services you use and earn a competitive earnings credit allowance to offset fees
