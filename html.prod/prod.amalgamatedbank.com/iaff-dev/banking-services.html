It's easy to bank when and where you want

We know you need easy, fast and reliable access to your money 24/7. We make sure you have that, and more.

Convenient access to your money

No matter where you are, you always have access to your accounts.

  • ATMs nationwide — access over 40,000 surcharge-free Allpoint® ATMs across the country.1
    To find the nearest ATM, use the ATM Locator tool at the top of this page or download the Allpoint app.
  • IAFF-FC Debit MasterCard® — get cash and make purchases at millions of locations worldwide.

Mobile Banking2

Now there's nothing to hold you back from banking where you want, when you want. All you need is the free Amalgamated Bank mobile app and your iPhone,® iPad,® AndroidTM or SMS text device.2

  • View account information
  • Transfer funds3
  • Make deposits
  • Pay bills
  • Find the nearest Amalgamated branch or Allpoint ATM
  • Get helpful mobile alerts

Online Banking

Access your Amalgamated Bank accounts online anytime, anywhere.

  • View your account balances and transaction history
  • Pay bills and schedule bills to be paid automatically
  • Transfer funds between your Amalgamated Bank accounts, as well as between your Amalgamated Bank accounts and your other account(s) held at other U.S. institutions3
  • Use People Pay to send money from your Amalgamated Bank account to another person using his or her email address or cell phone number3
  • View and print copies of canceled checks
  • Reorder checks, place stop payments3
