Custody Services

Our primary custody services include asset safekeeping, corporate actions, income collections, proxy services, account transition, asset transfer and conversion management.  Your organization will have at its disposal a dedicated customer service team that knows and understands your account and a Relationship Manager who will act as a single point of contact.

Retirement Plans

Our Defined Contribution Plans draw on our specialized expertise and technological resources to provide a comprehensive service platform for individual pension solutions. We work closely with you to customize a cost-effective service program, coordinate every aspect of your plan, and give you access to 500+ mutual fund families and 200,000 funds, including mutual funds, ETFs and collective trusts.

Keith Mestrich: President & CEO

Keith joined the bank in 2012 after serving four years as the CFO of SEIU. A 25-year veteran of the labor movement with Workers United, UNITE HERE, UNITE and the AFL-CIO, he started at Amalgamated as head of the D.C. region. In addition to working at the bank, Keith serves as Chair of the Board of Directors of DC Employment Justice Center and Treasurer of the Union Health Center in NYC and he serves on the board of the National Consumers League.

Samantha Berg: Executive Vice President, Head of Community Banking

Samantha is a marketing and servicing leader with twenty years in banking. Prior to Amalgamated, she headed Small Business Marketing and was Chief of Staff for the Commercial Bank at Capital One. In addition, she has managed Agent Bank relationships for OPEN at American Express and held senior product, marketing and servicing positions at Chase. Samantha serves on the board of Women’s Initiative of New York. Her history with Amalgamated began with her great grandfather, a sewing machine operator, and union member.

Joseph E. Brunken: Senior Vice President, Head of Investment Management Division

Joe joined the bank as Director of Marketing & Client Service in 2010. A 30 year veteran in financial services, he has prior experience at Mutual of America Capital Management Corporation where he oversaw all sales and marketing activities and Nikko Capital where he raised $3B.

Lawrence Fruchtman: Executive Vice President, General Counsel

Prior to joining Amalgamated, Larry served as Special Counsel at Cadwalader, Vice-President and Counsel at Natwest Bancorp, and started career at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. He is the author of “Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act of 1999, Financial Services Modernization: Risks and Opportunities for Insurers Under the New Affiliation Rules”.

Mark Minter: Executive Vice President, Chief Credit Risk Officer

A seasoned credit and risk executive, Mark joined Amalgamated from RBS Citizens Financial Group, where he was Head of Corporate Banking Commercial Portfolio Management, and Chief Credit Officer (CCO) - Wholesale. Mark began his career in banking at JPMorgan Chase where he held lending positions in Textiles and Apparel, Foreign Direct Investment, and Electronics and High Tech. He subsequently held management roles in Credit, Leveraged Buyouts, and Loan Workout at Citibank and at IBJ Whitehall Bank. Mark served on the board of Project Renewal, a nonprofit organization that provides housing and job training for homeless individuals in New York City.

Kenneth Schmidt: Interim Chief Financial Officer

Ken joined Amalgamated in 2012 as Treasurer, with over 20 years of banking experience. He began his career in Investment Accounting at the Dime Savings Bank of New York. He has served as Portfolio Manager for two international banks and most recently was Chief Financial Officer for Darien Rowayton Bank where Ken played a key role in returning the bank to key regulatory standing and profitability.

Brian Smalley: Senior Vice President, Commercial Real Estate

Prior to joining the bank in 2010, Brian served as Vice President of Commercial Real Estate at M&T Bank. Brian specializes in multi-family buildings, cooperative underlying mortgages, bulk cooperative and condominium apartments, mixed-use properties, and office buildings. His career began at the NYC Housing Partnership, promoting the development of affordable housing in NYC.

Ben Szwalbenest: Executive Vice President, Chief Administrative Officer & Chief Compliance Officer

Ben joined Amalgamated Bank in November 2011 as the bank's Director of Regulatory Compliance, bringing with him over 30 years of diversified banking experience. Ben previously served as a Managing Director at JP Morgan Chase, leading the heritage Bear Stearns domestic bank, Custodial Trust Company. Prior to his time at Bear Stearns and Custodial Trust Company, Ben served as a Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System commissioned Senior Bank Examiner at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, where he managed the compliance team enforcing consumer compliance, margin lending, and anti-money laundering laws and regulations.
